Dr M Nagi Reddy

Chairman and Managing Director
MBBS.,MRCP., MRCGP., MSc., MBA.,LLb.

An Endocrinologist, Diabetologist, and General Physician, Dr. M Nagi Reddy graduated (MBBS) from the prestigious Gandhi Medical College, Bassherbagh, Hyderabad. He obtained higher medical qualifications in General Medicine, Diabetes & Endocrinology from UK. He holds membership of the Prestigious Royal Colleges in the United Kingdom - MRCP (UK), and MRCGP (UK).
